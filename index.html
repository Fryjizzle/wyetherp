Walking the Forest Path

Most technology feels cold and rushed—like walking through a tunnel. I like the forest path instead, where things move slowly and care about how you feel.

What I Believe

I think every website or app is a chance to connect with someone. Code can help people—or make things harder. I don’t just build software. I build digital spaces where people who think differently feel at home.

Listening First

Before I start anything, I ask, “What does this want to be?” Whether I’m working on Crumb (my gentle AI friend) or building a website, I try to hear the quiet things most tech ignores. The best ideas often speak softly.

Code With Care

Coding is like meditation to me. I write each function with a purpose. I place buttons where they feel right. I follow a simple truth: if something feels good, it usually works well too. The strongest tech is often the softest.

The Five Elements

I use five elements to guide me:

  • 🔥 Fire – Inspiration and passion
  • 🌊 Water – Smooth, easy flow
  • 🌬️ Air – Clear and kind communication
  • 🪨 Earth – Solid, steady foundations
  • ✨ Aether – The big vision that ties it all together

My Mission

I’m not here to build the fastest or fanciest apps. I want to make safe and thoughtful spaces—where all kinds of minds feel welcome. Most tech is built for one kind of brain. I build for the ones that don’t fit the mold.
