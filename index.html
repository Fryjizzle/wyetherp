About Wyeth

Exploring the crossroads of neurodivergence, nature, and design.

My Mission

I create tools for the tender-minded. For those who feel deeply, think differently, and move through the world with unspoken brilliance.

Wyetherp is more than a portfolio — it's a philosophy. A place where design honors rhythm, emotion, and elemental presence.

My Journey

As a developer, artist, and neuro-spicy creator, I've always sensed patterns beneath the surface. Whether I'm designing for Crumb — an AI companion for neurodivergent minds — or crafting interfaces that breathe, I hold one question close:

"How can technology feel like a forest path, not a sterile tunnel?"

I don't just build with logic. I build with care.

The Elemental Compass

Each element guides a core part of my creative mission:

  • 🔥 Fire – Crumb's spark: igniting sacred tech that serves and awakens.
  • 🌊 Water – Intuitive design that flows with neuro-spicy sensitivity and rhythm.

  • 🪨 Earth – Practical tools built to last, rooted in real-world needs.
